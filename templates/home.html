AI Crowd Panic Detection System

Advanced AI-powered system for predicting and preventing panic situations in crowded environments using computer vision technology.


PanicVision is an Artificial Intelligence-based crowd monitoring system designed to detect unusual crowd density levels from uploaded images and videos. The system automatically classifies crowd conditions into Low, Medium, High, and Panic Alert levels to support safety monitoring in public environments.

🚦 Crowd Alert Levels

  • LOW CROWD → Safe Condition
  • MEDIUM CROWD → Moderate Density
  • HIGH CROWD → Warning Level
  • PANIC ALERT → Immediate Action Required 🚨
