💡 How Video Detection Works

📤 1. Upload a video file (MP4, AVI, MOV, MKV)

🤖 2. AI processes each frame using YOLOv8 object detection

📊 3. System tracks peak crowd density throughout the video

🚨 4. Automatic alerts trigger if panic threshold is exceeded

💾 5. Results saved to logs with processed video output

📊 Alert Level Thresholds

🟢 LOW CROWD: (Safe - No action needed)

🟡 MEDIUM CROWD: (Moderate - Monitor situation)

🟠 HIGH CROWD: (Warning - Prepare for action)

🔴 PANIC ALERT: (Emergency - Immediate action required!)

💡 Tips for Best Results

🎥 Use clear videos with good lighting

📏 Ensure people are visible and not too small in frame

⏱️ Shorter videos process faster (recommended under 2 minutes)

📹 Higher resolution videos take longer to process
